13  """ This light type simulates a PointLight. It has a position
14  and a radius. The attenuation is computed based on a quadratic
15  function.
16 
17  Shadows are simulated using 2 Parabolic maps. So this light has
18  2 Shadow maps, and when calling setShadowMapResolution() you are
19  actually setting the resolution for both maps. This is still
20  cheaper than a cubemap, though, while mainting almost the same
21  quality.
22 
23  Both shadow cameras are a bit displaced (by self.spacing),
24  as a parabolic map does not cover a full 180 degree view. Also,
25  the farPlane of both sources is shifted by self.bufferRadius,
26  because the precision is really bad near the outer radius.
